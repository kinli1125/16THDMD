Red Signal

Virtual Reality Experience / 彩色 Colour / No dialogue and subtitles 無對白及字幕
Takila Studio
沈家緯
吳嘉進
黃泓清
王瑀澤
林詠順
蔣承軒
Installation - VR 裝置
On average, 4.5 people die in accidents in Taiwan every day. Often, however, modern road safety advertising only consists of charts or data which are detached from reality and lack a sense of solidarity and therefore do not reach many people. The interactive design Red Signal aims to raise people’s awareness. It simulates car accidents, depicted on real Taiwanese roads, and illustrates life after death, combining virtual reality, 360-degree video technology and hardware. The objective of the work is to make users become afraid of car accidents and take traffic safety more seriously.
台灣交通意外發生頻繁，平均每天4.5人喪命。而現階段社會上的交通宣導多為 數據或圖表，對大多數人而言缺少真實感和貼近感。因此設計一款模擬車禍以 及死後世界的互動設計”RED SIGNAL” 此互動設計結合了虛擬實境和360度影 片以及硬體裝置，在美術設定上模擬了台灣現實的街道，在跨領域的結合後強 化了真實感。希望能利用此互動設計使體驗者感受到車禍的恐懼並更注重交通 安全。

贖罪亭

2D / Manga / MV / Computer Graphics 電腦繪圖

動畫組
CHI-CHIA LIN
林其加
As for the evil ones, the way we judged from various media from an early age is mostly extreme. There are practical harms, and I don’t know how to repent to ridiculously. But what is terrible than these bad guys? It is those naive humans. Their childishness often hurts sincere people. It is like an incomplete treatment, or an inferiority.
